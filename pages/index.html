A&R Styles

A&R Styles is an e-commerce platform specializing in clothing, offering a user-friendly interface to browse and purchase various clothing items. The website utilizes the H&M API to provide real-time information on clothing availability and prices in AUD.

Features:
Browse All Clothes: Users can explore a comprehensive catalog of available clothing items directly from the homepage. Search Functionality: Search for specific types of clothing using keywords or phrases to quickly locate desired items. Filter by Categories: Users can filter clothing items based on categories such as Women, Men, Kids, and Baby to streamline their shopping experience. Sort by Price: Clothing items can be sorted by price to help users find items that fit their budget preferences. View Cloth Details: Detailed information about each clothing item is available upon clicking, providing users with essential details before making a purchase decision. Add to Shopping Bag: Users can add desired clothing items to their shopping bag directly from the browsing or detailed view. Shopping Bag Management: Manage items in the shopping bag, including modifying quantities and removing items as needed before proceeding to checkout. Checkout Process: Seamless checkout process to finalize purchases, ensuring a smooth transaction experience for users.
